How to Play Surfing2048

1

Swipe in any direction to move all tiles. Tiles with the same number will merge when they collide.

2

After each move, a new tile (either 2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot.

3

Merge tiles to create higher numbers. Reach the 2048 tile to complete the basic challenge!

4

Special beach tiles appear at higher levels - merge them for bonus points and to unlock surfers.
