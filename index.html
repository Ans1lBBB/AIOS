The AIOS Legend

In the mythical AIOS realm, ocean goddesses maintained world balance until a catastrophe shattered their divine forms and scattered their memories across the sea. As their power faded, the ocean began sinking into the abyss.

An ancient prophecy foretold that when memory fragments return to the temple, the sleeping goddesses will awaken. As the chosen Memory Traveler, you must reassemble their fragments and uncover the truth behind the AIOS realm.

Your Mission

Help the reincarnated goddesses rebuild their shattered memories and recover their true identities. When all memories are restored, the lost "Core of Divinity" will awaken, revealing the final truth about the AIOS realm.
